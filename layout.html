A guitar is a popular musical instrument classified as a string instrument with anywhere from 4 to 18 strings, usually having 6. The sound is projected either acoustically or through amplification. It is typically played by strumming or plucking the strings with the right hand while fretting the strings with the left hand.

Trey Anastasio is a founding member of the rock band Phish, serving as lead guitarist and vocalist since their inception. Phish are noted for their musical improvisation, extended jams, exploration of a broad range of genre, and original live performances. Formed at the U of Vermont in 1983, the band includes bassist and vocalist Mike Gordon, Percussionist Jon Fishman, and Keyboardist Paige McConnell.
